Some tips for your first interview:

  1. Listen carefully to what the interviewer asks, ask for clarifying questions.
  2. Prepare youself for technical round/coding round by practicing enough competitive programming questions
  3. Think out loud, don't keep silent for long. If you need a few seconds to think, let the interviewer know.
  4. Always be honest. Do what you are capable of. Do not find ways to cheat.
  5. While solving a question, interviewers often drop hints. Make sure you take them seriously
  6. Research about the role in advance, know what the expectations are, know about their culture.
  7. Have some sample answers already prepared for 'Why this company?', 'Why this role?', 'What is your career path?'li>
  8. Show commitment towards your role. Don't say you are joining for a few years/months.
  9. When talking about a project, be clear on what your contributions were and how it was fundamental in the bigger picture.
  10. Show that you take active initative and get things done.

Google STEP (Student Training in Engineering Program)

STEP is a 12-week internship for students with a passion for computer science. The internship program has a focus of providing development opportunities to students from groups historically underrepresented in tech, through technical training and professional development.

Applications Open: Different times of the year for different places. Starts from September tentatively
Applications Close: Different times of the year for different places
Eligibiblity: First and second-year undergraduate that have taken atleast 1-2 college level computer science courses

Internship through Microsoft Codess

Codess is a community for female coders initiated by Microsoft. It is established to explore ways to promote gender diversity in the engineering field. For Indians, Microsoft holds an online coding test in the month of February to invite women to their annual Codess event held in Hyderabad/Banglore. People invited to the event have a chance of getting recruited as a summer intern at Microsoft

Applications Open: January
Applications Close: February
Eligibiblity: Second-year Indian female undergraduate students pursuing computer science or related fields.
