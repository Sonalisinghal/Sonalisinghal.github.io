Google CodeJam I/0 for Women

Code Jam to I/O for Women is organised to bring women from all around the world to solve tough algorithmic challenges in a 2.5 hour, single-round coding competition.The top 150 on the scoreboard will receive a ticket and reimbursement to offset travel expenses to Google I/O.

Applications Open: January
Team Size: 1

Google CodeJam

Code Jam is Google's longest running global coding competition. Competitors work their way through a series of online algorithmic puzzles to earn a spot at the World Finals, all for a chance to win the championship title and $15,000.

Applications Open: March
Team Size: 1
Eligibiblity: 18 years or older

Google Hashcode

Hash Code is a team programming competition, organized by Google, for students and professionals around the world. Pick your team and programming language and solve some interesting engineering problems.Top teams will then be invited to a Google office for the Final Round.
