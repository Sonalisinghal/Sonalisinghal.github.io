Competitive Programming

Competitive programming is an essential skill to have if you want to land a Software Engineering job. It is the most common way for companies to judge your quick thinking and problem-solving skills.

Some tips to get started with Competitive programming:

  1. Be fluent in the programming language of your choice.
  2. Learn about space and time complexity.
  3. Learn about data structures and algorithms.
  4. It becomes better with practice. Practice regularly any of the practicing platforms (Find them below).
  5. Participate in online coding contests frequently to build speed.
  6. Always look at editorials of a problem. They are useful in learning how to solve a problem efficiently and how to write good code. Then try to implement it yourself.
  7. Don't be afraid to participate or let your rank go down in contest. Learning curve is a little steep at the start since there are many concepts to learn. It's normal. Just keep practicing and soon you'll start enjoying it :)
  8. Refer to books, videos and blogs online to help you get started.

Code Jam is Google's longest running global coding competition. Competitors work their way through a series of online algorithmic puzzles to earn a spot at the World Finals, all for a chance to win the championship title and $15,000.
