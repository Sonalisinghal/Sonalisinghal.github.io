Scholarships

Scholarships to promote women in technology and academics

AnitaB.org Grace hopper Scholarship

The GHC Scholars Program provides funds for women who are either undergraduate students, graduate students, or faculty to attend Grace Hopper Celebration.

Applications Open: January
Applications Close: March
Eligibiblity: For students: Women, 18+ by September of that year. Full-time student enrolled in an accredited degree program at a college or university pursuing Computer Science/Engineering or related fields.
Benefits: Scholarship covers the cost of individual registeration for the GHC, hotel accomodation, airfare and stipend (to be used for travel, meals, and incidentals such as rideshares, baggage fees, etc.)

AnitaB.org Pass it on awards

The cash award helps fund women in computing or projects that inspire and support girls and women to enter computing. Recipients are encouraged to "pass on" the benefits they gain from the award, creating a movement of women helping women.

Applications Open: January
Applications Close: March
Eligibiblity: Any woman over 18 may apply, though she must be in or aspiring to be in computing fields
Benefits: Awards range up to $1,000 USD

Adobe Women In tech Scholarship

Aim towards creating gender equality in science, technology and engineering domains by encouraging women to showcase their excellence in computing and technology and become future leaders and role models in the field.

Applications Open: August
Applications Close: September
Eligibiblity: Be an Indian female citizen student,enrolled as a full time student in a formal technology 4 year BE / B.Tech education program or an Integrated ME/MS/MTech program at an Indian University, completing the program next year. Should be pursuing a Major or Minor in Computer Science/Engineering or related fields.
Benefits: Tution fee for remainder of recipient's education, Opportunity for summer internship at Adobe India, Mentoring by a senior technology leader from Adobe, Travel to Grace Hopper Conference India, including participation fees.
